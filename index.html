About Rosa

Rosa is from Lima, Perú. After traveling throughout her own country and other parts of South America working in the film and video industry, Rosa moved to Paris, France, where she graduated in screenwriting. She then worked in the Dominican Republic, an experience that helped to enriched her vocabulary—every Spanish-speaking country has its own language codes. Back in Peru, she met a wonderful man from Minnesota and relocated with him to Minneapolis where they were married. After working eight years as a translator/captioner at CaptionMax, she decided to take advantage of her travel experiences and communication skills to build what she considers a bridge between the cultures that she knows and loves through good old-fashioned conversations. Besides Spanish and English, Rosa also knows French and Italian. "This is a global community. A second language will always open doors for you. Don´t let that treasure go to waste."
