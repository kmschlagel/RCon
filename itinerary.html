Perú Itinerary

Lima, Lambayeque, Chachapoyas: Cocktail of Cultures.

This is a less traveled road in Perú where we will be able to appreciate the habitat of some pre-Inca cultures and the greatness of this beautiful country and its people. English is less spoken in these areas—even guides might only speak Spanish—and because you can only arrive to Chachapoyas by bus, there are not as many tourists there as in other parts of the country. So to speak and/or understand some Spanish is advised for this tour even if we'll be there for you.

There are some places my husband and I will discover with you and some that we have especially explored beforehand for you. This is the way we travel ourselves every time we go to my native country, discovering every time new treasures even in places we thought we knew. So we hope you will enjoy our trips as much as we do.

We stay mostly at two- three-star hotels. And just so you know, Perú can be as expensive as the US. We will meet in Lima Feb. 20th—when we will pick you at the airport and lodge you—to start the tour the following day.
